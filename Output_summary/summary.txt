The appellants were displaced persons who had migrated to Pakistan, having entered into an agreement with the respondent under section 8(1) of Evacuee Property Act 1952 for lease on certain properties they owned. On 30 August 1952, a notice was issued by Mr Abdul Karim and his brother Messrs Rameshwar Nath & Rajinder Narain in which he alleged that there would not be any action against them as their property vested within Section 12(2) (XXXI) or (4)(A). He also stated: "The Respondent has no authority over these matters."<n>On appeal from this order dated 31 March 1954 it held; 'No such power exists'
